About Me

I am a 4th year undergraduate student at The Ohio State University. I will be graduating in May 2020 with an Honors Bachelors of Science with a double major in Computer Science and Mathematics. I have done research in some combinatorics including graph theory and knot theory, but now my interest lies primarily with artificial intelligence. The main research I have done currently is investigating the trade-off between specificity and accuracy in the context of computer vision.
