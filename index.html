About Me

I am a 4th year undergraduate student at The Ohio State University. I will be graduating in May 2020 with an Honors Bachelors of Science for a double major in Computer Science and Mathematics. I have previously done research in combinatorics including graph theory and knot theory, but now my interest lies primarily with artificial intelligence and machine learning. In particular, I have a strong interest in investigating methods with connections to deep learning; for more details on this interest, visit my research tab on this site.
